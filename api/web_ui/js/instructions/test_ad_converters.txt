  <li class="verify-potentiometer">Verify that a 20 kΩ potentiometer is connected to External Thermistor Connector 1.</li>
  <li class="rotate-potentiometer">Rotate the potentiometer knob to its full range and check for resistance values near both 0 Ω and 20 kΩ.</li>
  <li class="smooth-transition">Ensure a smooth transition of resistance across the range (e.g., 1 kΩ, 5 kΩ, 10 kΩ, 15 kΩ, up to 20 kΩ).</li>
  <li class="verify-potentiometer">Repeat the above steps for External Thermistor Connector 2.</li>
</ol>
